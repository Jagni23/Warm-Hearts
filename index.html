Our Partner NGOs

We partner with a diverse group of non-governmental organizations dedicated to various causes. You can choose one of them for your donation, or we can help match it with the most suitable one.

  • Hope Foundation

    Focuses on providing food, shelter, and medical aid to homeless individuals and families.

  • Shelter for All

    Dedicated to building safe and sustainable housing for low-income communities.

  • Education First

    Supports educational initiatives, providing books, scholarships, and school supplies to underprivileged children.

  • Green Earth Alliance

    Works on environmental conservation, tree planting, and promoting sustainable practices.

  • Community Care Network

    Aims to strengthen local communities through various outreach programs and support services.
